Must-Visit Kenyan Destinations

Wildlife & Safari

  • Maasai Mara: The Great Migration (July-Oct)
  • Amboseli: Elephants with Kilimanjaro views
  • Tsavo East/West: Red elephants & diverse landscapes
  • Samburu: Rare species like Grevy's zebra

Beaches & Coastal

  • Diani Beach: White sands & coral reefs
  • Watamu: Marine parks & turtle nesting
  • Lamu Island: Swahili culture & donkeys
  • Malindi: Italian influence & deep-sea fishing

Cultural & Other

  • Nairobi: Giraffe Centre & Karen Blixen
  • Lake Nakuru: Flamingos & rhinos
  • Hell's Gate: Cycling safari & geothermal spa
  • Mount Kenya: Hiking & alpine scenery

Travel Tips for Kenya

  1. Visa: Apply for an e-visa before arrival
  2. Vaccinations: Yellow fever certificate may be required
  3. Currency: Kenyan Shilling (carry small bills)
  4. Safety: Avoid walking alone at night in cities
